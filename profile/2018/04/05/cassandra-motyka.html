Cassandra Motyka

Hi everyone! My name is Cassandra Motyka, and I’m a freshman here at Penn State in the Schreyer Honors College. I’m majoring in Security and Risk Analysis, and I am pursuing minors in Spanish and Global Security. One day I hope to work for the World Health Organization and track the movement of viruses and diseases and help stop their spread. I joined F.O.R.M. consultants because I wanted to help students like me that went to large high schools have more one-on-one support when applying to colleges. Here at Penn State outside of F.O.R.M., I am the captain of a Mock Trial team and the secretary of College Independents. I am excited to work with everyone and see all of the great things our organization and the mentees can accomplish!
