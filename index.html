About Us

We are a small mentorship organization that seeks to provide free services to the underprivileged high-school student demographic, ranging from students seeking elite schools to students seeking alternative higher education options. We leverage mentors’ existing talents and perspectives along with services like essay tutoring, individualized college counseling, and more, to maximize mentees’ potential and achieve their goals.

Mission

To mentor and assist students in a professional and advantageous way so that they can be their college best, regardless of their socioeconomic status or race.
